Candidates

Becoming a candidate is a 2-Step process – Registration and Nomination. Only candidates that have completed both steps will be listed on the ballot.

Step 1: Register

All Candidates must complete the registration process under the Election Finances and Contributions Disclosure Act. This process provides Elections Alberta with information on the potential candidate, their Chief Financial Officer, location of records and their financial institution for their accounts for contributions and expenses for their campaign. This process starts the financial component of the candidate’s campaign and information on candidates that are at this stage of the process can be found at https://efpublic.elections.ab.ca/efCandidatesPGE.cfm?MID=FC_2023&OFSFID=101&EDS=ALL

Step 2: Nomination

All Candidates must be nominated for election by 25 electors in the electoral division. All nomination signatures must be collected in person and be accompanied by the elector’s name and physical address. Nomination papers must be submitted to the Returning Officer by 2:00 pm on Nomination Day, Thursday, May 11, 2023 for review and acceptance.
